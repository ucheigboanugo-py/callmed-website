Subscription Offer

Offer available to new users who activate an eligible CallMed device. ₦4,500/month after the trial period. Only one offer per CallMed account and one offer per household if you’re part of a family group, regardless of the number of devices purchased. This offer is not valid if you or your household have previously subscribed to CallMed+. Offer valid for 3 months after the activation of an eligible device. Subscription automatically renews until canceled. Restrictions and other terms apply. A subscription is required to access CallMed+. CallMed+ requires CallMed Orbit or a compatible device paired with the CallMed Linq app.

Trade-In Program

Trade-in values will vary based on the condition, year, and configuration of your eligible trade-in device. Not all devices are eligible for credit. You must be at least 18 years old to trade in for credit or a CallMed Gift Card. Trade-in value can be applied toward the purchase of a qualifying new device or added to a CallMed Gift Card. The actual value awarded depends on the receipt of a qualifying device matching the description provided when the estimate was made. Sales tax may apply to the full value of a new device purchase. In-store trade-ins require a valid photo ID (local laws may require saving this information). Offer availability may vary between in-store and online trade-ins. Some stores may have additional requirements. CallMed or its trade-in partners reserve the right to refuse, cancel, or limit trade-in transactions for any reason. More details about the trade-in and recycling of eligible devices are available from CallMed’s trade-in partner. Restrictions and limitations may apply.

CallMed Intelligence Availability

CallMed Intelligence, an AI-driven health analytics feature, is available in beta on CallMed Orbit, CallMed Linq, and CallMed Core platforms. Additional features and languages will roll out throughout the year. Supported languages include English (Nigeria, UK, US, South Africa), French, Portuguese, and Spanish.

CallMed Pay Terms

To access and use all CallMed Pay features, users must add their CallMed Pay Card to a compatible device running Wear OS or paired with the CallMed Linq app. CallMed Pay is subject to approval and is available only to qualifying applicants. CallMed Pay services are facilitated in partnership with licensed financial institutions.
