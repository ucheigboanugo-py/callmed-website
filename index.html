CallMed+ Subscription Offer

New users activating an eligible CallMed service are entitled to a special introductory offer. Enjoy ₦5,000/month after the trial period. Each CallMed account is eligible for one offer per household if part of a family group, regardless of the number of activations. This offer is not available to users or households who have previously subscribed to CallMed+. The offer is valid for 3 months after activation and renews automatically unless canceled. A subscription is required to access CallMed+ services.

CallMed Pay & Rewards Program

CallMed Pay is an integrated digital payment system within the CallMed Linq ecosystem, allowing seamless transactions for healthcare services, pharmacy purchases, and wellness subscriptions. Users who sign up for CallMed Pay enjoy:

  • Instant Access to partner hospitals, pharmacies, and HMO services.
  • 3% Cashback on eligible transactions, encouraging cost-effective healthcare spending.
  • Loyalty Rewards for consistent usage, redeemable for discounts, consultations, or premium services.
  • Peer-to-Peer Transfers enabling users to send funds to family and friends for in-app purchases.

CallMed Intelligence

CallMed Intelligence is an AI-driven health analytics engine designed to enhance patient care by offering:

  • Predictive analytics for early disease detection.
  • Personalized health recommendations based on real-time health data.
  • Seamless integration with CallMed Linq for real-time monitoring and insights.

Currently available in beta, CallMed Intelligence supports English (Nigeria, UK, US, South Africa), French, Portuguese, and Spanish, with more languages rolling out soon.
