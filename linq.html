Meet CallMed Linq

CallMed Linq is a revolutionary telehealth platform that connects patients with certified doctors for remote consultations, medical advice, and prescriptions—all from the comfort of their homes. With built-in features like electronic health records, in-app drug purchases, fitness tracking, lab test bookings, and an AI-powered heart disease prediction tool, CallMed Linq makes healthcare more accessible, affordable, and efficient.

Designed for convenience and reliability, CallMed Linq ensures that quality healthcare is just a tap away, eliminating the need for unnecessary hospital visits while prioritizing patient well-being.
